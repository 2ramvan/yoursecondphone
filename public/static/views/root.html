Video Chat

Your Second Phone allows you to chat face-to-face with anyone. Simply create a session, send your friend the link and chat for as long as you need, it's free!

 No Account

You don't need to have an account to use Your Second Phone. Once a session is created, the link to it will last for 24 hours, but the call can last much longer.

Step 1: Allow access to webcam!

Your Second Phone needs access to your webcam, click the button below to allow access.

Step 2: Pick a name for the conversation!

Here you can pick a specific name for your conversation, this makes you easier to find. If you don't want to pick a name, that is okay, you'll just get a random one.
